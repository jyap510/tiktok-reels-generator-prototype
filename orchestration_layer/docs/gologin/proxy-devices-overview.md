# Proxy Devices

**Proxy Devices** lets you route your browser profile traffic through your own Android phone instead of a traditional proxy server.

In simple terms, your phone becomes the internet connection for your profile.

## Why use it?

This feature is useful when you want:

* A **real mobile IP address** (instead of datacenter or residential proxies)
* A \*\*more trustworthy IP \*\*for websites
* To reduce reliance on third-party proxy providers
* To appear as a **real mobile user** from your device’s network

## How it works?

* You install a companion app on your Android device
* The app shares your phone’s internet connection
* Your profile connects to that device as a proxy
* All traffic goes through your phone’s IP

<Warning>
  Do not use the same proxy device on different profiles at the same time. The IP will be the same, which is suspicious for websites.
</Warning>

## Setup guide

### Install the Gologin Devices mobile app

1. **Download and install** the Gologin Devices app from Google Play on your Android phone
2. **Log in** to your Gologin account
3. Press **Start proxy**

### Continue in the Gologin Desktop app

1. In the Gologin app, **go to Proxy Devices** and verify the new device has appeared after you've logged in on Android
