# Extra FAQ — too obvious to ask, too important to ignore

## Do I need to change any settings?

If the profile is already created and the location is selected, no extra actions are needed. Just start the profile and use it like a regular browser.

## I created a profile and added a proxy. Is that all?

Yes, after that, you can start the profile and use it.

## Do I have to use a proxy?

Yes, we recommend using a unique proxy for each profile. This is important for security. Gologin provides built-in residential proxies with 2 GB of data included in each paid plan. The trial plan includes 500 MB of residential proxies

## Can I open different websites in the same profile?

Yes, you can open different websites in one profile. Each profile acts like a separate device, so you can use it just like a normal browser.

## Can multiple people use the same profile at the same time?

No, we have a setting that prevents a profile from being opened twice. This helps keep your account secure and prevents multiple users from accessing the same profile at the same time.

## Can I open multiple profiles at once?

Yes, you can open as many profiles as your computer can handle. Usually, even weak computers can run up to 10 profiles without issues.

## Can my virtual assistant continue from where I left off?

Yes, your virtual assistant will see the same tabs, stay logged into the same accounts, and have access to the browser history.

## Will my tabs stay open?

Yes, tabs will be saved.

## Will I stay logged in?

Yes, you will stay logged in.

## Will cookies be saved?

Yes, cookies will be saved.

## Is everything included in the plan?

Yes, all paid plans include 2 GB of high-quality residential proxies. The trial plan includes 500 MB of residential proxies. All other features, including fingerprint spoofing, are also included in the paid plan and available in the trial version.

## What is the difference between sharing a profile and inviting someone to a team?

When you share a profile, the person sees only that profile in their workspace and cannot access any other profiles.

When you invite someone to your team, they see all your profiles. If you want them to see only specific profiles, you can add them to a folder instead. However, they will still be part of your team. When sharing a profile, they work in their own workspace and only see the profile you shared with them.

## What is the difference between workspaces and folders?

Workspaces contain folders. Folders are inside workspaces.

## Do Chrome extensions work?

Yes, all Chrome Store extensions work in Gologin. You can add them from our catalog or upload your own extension.

## What is the best way to install extensions?

You can install extensions through the profile settings or upload your own manually.
