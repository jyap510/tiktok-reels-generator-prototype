# View and top up proxy traffic

## How to view available proxy traffic

**To view** the available Gologin proxy traffic, just click **Buy proxy** in the **top-right corner** of the Gologin app window.
You will see a list of **all available proxy types** and the **remaining traffic amount** for each of them.

<img src="https://mintcdn.com/gologin/IqACfYC0Ic7KlHQ4/images/GoLogin_view_traffic.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=IqACfYC0Ic7KlHQ4&q=85&s=1d46a6eab3bd58bae6586032c1fe52ea" alt="GoLogin_view_traffic.png" width="1183" height="748" data-path="images/GoLogin_view_traffic.png" />

## How to top up traffic

If you need more traffic, you can purchase it in just a few steps:

1. Click **Buy proxy**
2. Click **Top up** next to the proxy type you want to add traffic to
3. **Select the traffic amount**
   The required amount depends on your use case. It mostly varies based on:
   * How often you use your browser profiles
   * How long your sessions last
   * How much heavy content (such as videos, images, or audio) you load while working
4. **Choose a payment method**
   We accept **Credit Card** and **Cryptocurrency** payments for proxy traffic.
5. **Complete the payment** in the browser window

<img src="https://mintcdn.com/gologin/IqACfYC0Ic7KlHQ4/images/GoLogin_buy_proxy.gif?s=16ed51de9d8670bd8e0f6fce38c6208a" alt="GoLogin_buy_proxy.gif" width="1183" height="748" data-path="images/GoLogin_buy_proxy.gif" />

Once the payment is completed, the traffic will be **added automatically** to your account and available for use right away.

## Adding proxy traffic into Gologin subscription

If you have a general idea of how much **residential proxy traffic** you need each month, you can add it directly to your subscription. This allows you to **avoid manual top-ups** and ensures that proxy traffic is added automatically on a monthly basis.

To add the Gologin proxy traffic into your subscription:

1. Open the **main menu** and go to **Settings & Members**.
2. Navigate to **Billing plans**.
3. Click **Change plan**.
4. **Select the required amount** of proxy traffic that will be added to your account every month.
5. **Complete the payment** in the browser window.

<img src="https://mintcdn.com/gologin/IqACfYC0Ic7KlHQ4/images/GoLogin_proxy-to-subscription.gif?s=b7ea7e856e7f9b7a5550cde278539af3" alt="GoLogin_proxy-to-subscription.gif" width="1183" height="748" data-path="images/GoLogin_proxy-to-subscription.gif" />

If you don't use all your proxy traffic from the previous month, the unused traffic will **carry over** and be added to the new monthly amount.
Gologin proxy traffic does not expire.
