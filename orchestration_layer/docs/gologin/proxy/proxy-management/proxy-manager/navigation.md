# Navigation

### Adding a proxy

To add a single or multiple proxies, copy your proxy > hover over the **Location** column on your profile > click **Paste proxies** or **Add proxy** button > use the **hot keys** to paste the proxies.

### Checking a proxy

To check a proxy, just **click the arrows** next to the proxy name on the All Profiles list.\
It will show the proxy country and the current outgoing IP.

<img src="https://mintcdn.com/gologin/icD2z3poOwslds91/images/GoLogin_Beta_LsbfxQHEdR.gif?s=785c1f2506744fd51623a183331e9e91" alt="GoLogin_Beta_LsbfxQHEdR.gif" width="1183" height="655" data-path="images/GoLogin_Beta_LsbfxQHEdR.gif" />

### Editing proxy settings

To edit the proxy settings such as IP or password, go to the proxy manager > **click the pencil icon** on the proxy you need to change > click **Save** after making changes.

<img src="https://mintcdn.com/gologin/icD2z3poOwslds91/images/GoLogin_Beta_BHHmuftQu4.gif?s=9ca9dd6cd1dbe5b93f0b49aeaed71fff" alt="GoLogin_Beta_BHHmuftQu4.gif" width="1183" height="655" data-path="images/GoLogin_Beta_BHHmuftQu4.gif" />

### Unlinking proxy from profile

To remove a proxy from a profile without deleting it, open the Proxy Manager by **clicking the proxy**, then **click the X to unlink** it.

<img src="https://mintcdn.com/gologin/icD2z3poOwslds91/images/GoLogin_Beta_A3HYv3sUXn.gif?s=7d25920c4b9da5ab641353b3354e9d6e" alt="GoLogin_Beta_A3HYv3sUXn.gif" width="1183" height="655" data-path="images/GoLogin_Beta_A3HYv3sUXn.gif" />

### Deleting a proxy

To delete a proxy, **click on any proxy** to open the Proxy Manager > **right-click** on the proxy you want to delete > click **Delete proxy** > **confirm** your choice.

<img src="https://mintcdn.com/gologin/icD2z3poOwslds91/images/GoLogin_Beta_zYWyP3x3hn.gif?s=83b19ce95842ca4c1e99ef470f320e28" alt="GoLogin_Beta_zYWyP3x3hn.gif" width="1183" height="655" data-path="images/GoLogin_Beta_zYWyP3x3hn.gif" />

### Copying proxies

To copy a proxy, **click on any proxy** to open the Proxy Manager > **right-click** on the proxy you want to copy > click **Copy proxy**.

<img src="https://mintcdn.com/gologin/icD2z3poOwslds91/images/GoLogin_Beta_M9PSGIHwCK.gif?s=e9805edfbd9d733e385a9cd236dfe688" alt="GoLogin_Beta_M9PSGIHwCK.gif" width="1183" height="655" data-path="images/GoLogin_Beta_M9PSGIHwCK.gif" />

### Bulk proxy actions

You can copy or delete **multiple proxies at a time**. Just click anywhere on the Location column to enter the Proxy Manager > **right-click on the proxy** > **Select proxy** > keep selecting the proxies you want to act on > click **Copy** or **Delete** at the bottom of the Proxy Manager window.
