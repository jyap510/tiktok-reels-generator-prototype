# Updating the Gologin app

If the update is available or already completed in the background, you will notice a button at the top of the window in your GoLogin app. It should look like this:

<Frame>
    <img src="https://mintcdn.com/gologin/wAzDTui6BFOkUOhm/images/image-12.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=wAzDTui6BFOkUOhm&q=85&s=eca6982141de4783d0af5a6997493157" alt="Image" width="1861" height="378" data-path="images/image-12.png" />

  If you see the notification, you just need to click the "Restart app" button and it's done, you are up to date!
</Frame>

In certain cases, for the older versions of the software especially (prior to 3.3.1), you may not be getting updates automatically. On this occasion, just download the latest version from [**<u>here</u>**](https://gologin.com/download). It will not result in any data loss as all your settings are securely stored in the cloud.
